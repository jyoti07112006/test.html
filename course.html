About BCA course

  • It is an undergraduate degree focused on computer applications, software development, and IT.

Duration of BCA

  • 4 yesr course with 8-Semesters

Eligibility

  • Must have completed 10th and 12th(or equivalent) from a recognized board.
  • some colleges prefer Mathematics at 12th level, but it is not always mandatory
  • Usually 45%–50% aggregate in 12th class (can vary by college/university).
  • Some universities or state-level exams may have an upper age limit (e.g., 21–25 years).
  • Basic computer knowledge is helpful but not mandatory.

main subjects

  • Database Management System
